Dr. Joe Jankowski

DOCTOR OF DENTAL MEDICINE
ADA AGD ISDS ICOI Fellow

"My goal is to get patients out of pain and provide effective care for a beautiful, healthy smile."

Dr. Jankowski earned his DMD from Southern Illinois School of Dental Medicine and completed a two-year post-doctoral residency in general dentistry, followed by a one-year fellowship in implant dentistry. He has been caring for patients in the Metro East since 1997.

His advanced training spans oral surgery, dental implants, endodontics, and restorative dentistry—giving his patients access to a full range of treatment options without the need for outside referrals.
